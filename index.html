Our Story

Founded in 2025, Royal Café began as a small coffee cart with big dreams. Today, we're proud to be a beloved neighborhood destination where people come together over exceptional coffee and food.

Our team of passionate baristas and chefs work with the finest ingredients to create memorable experiences in our warm, welcoming space. Every cup and plate is prepared with care and attention to detail.
